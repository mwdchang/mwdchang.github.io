Visualizing Entities in Text Documents with Descriptive NPR
Using non-photorealistic graphics, information visualization and natural language processing techniques to make sense of hundreds of thoudands of vehicle defect reports filed from NHTSA.
This application allows people to invetigate and explore quality/safety issues in intuitive manner by take advantage of real world metaphors.
This work is published in Pacific VIS 2013.
