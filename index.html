About
Hi there! I am Daniel Chang. I work as a principal research engineer, solving problems such as mixed-initiative approach for building predictive models, understanding unstructured streaming data (e.g. Twitter), geo-temporal data analytics, and interfaces for large touch displays. I am interested in Data Visualization, HCI and Machine Learning. This page showcases some of my personal projects and tinkerings. Always up for collaboration on interesting ideas and learning new things. Drop me a line at mwdchang@gmail.com.
